Building services

Providing high quality building and construction services

A wide range of professional building and construction contracting services for both domestic and commercial customers.

All trades covered

From groundwork to final finish

WJ Contracting can support projects from early planning through build, installation and decoration. Our team includes fully certified electricians and gas safe registered engineers, and all work is fully insured.

Full renovations

From simple modernisation to complete bare-shell rip-outs, with support on layouts, materials, suppliers and project management.

New builds

Practical support from the initial stages, covering all trades from the ground up to help bring new-build plans to life.

Extensions

Single-room additions, conservatories, orangeries and larger two-storey wrap-around extensions delivered with care.

Damp proofing

Solutions for rising and penetrating damp, including wall tanking, membranes and appropriate property protection.

Roofing

Maintenance and upgrades for flat and pitched felt, slate and tiled roofs to protect your property long term.

Steelwork

Experienced structural steel installation, from measurements and preparation through to making good and decoration.

Groundwork

Foundations, drainage, service installation and heavier digging works using machinery where required.

Patios & driveways

External improvements using concrete, block paving and chosen finishes to improve kerb appeal and daily use.

Kitchens

Kitchen projects from design-stage planning through plumbing, tiling, decorating and final finish.

Bathrooms

Bathrooms, en-suites and wet rooms planned around the best use of your space and a polished final result.

Plastering

Skimming, rendering and plaster replacement for smooth, even internal and external surfaces.

Brickwork

Brick and block work from small garden features to extensions, completed to a fully insured standard.

Painting & decorating

Interior woodwork, full exterior painting and larger decorative projects finished neatly and professionally.
